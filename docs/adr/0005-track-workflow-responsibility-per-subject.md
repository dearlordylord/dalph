# Track workflow responsibility per exact subject

Status: Accepted

Dalph tracks workflow responsibility independently for each task-coordination
action, workflow operation, and external resource rather than attaching one
responsibility flag to a whole planned task attempt. A fresh authority
observation may end Dalph's permission to act on one subject while obligations
for the same attempt remain—for example, a replaced tracker claim stops task
changes but does not erase the duty to preserve or safely dispose the worktree
and task-work session.

## Consequences

Every responsibility ends through its own completed disposition or durable
relinquishment event. Relinquishment records Dalph's changed obligation without
claiming ownership of external history. A task changed during implementation
prevents further state-changing work on that branch while its resources are
preserved; the later pause and reconciliation specifications decide the exact
operator choices and whether unpausing alone is ever sufficient.
